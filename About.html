About Emma's favourite books

Here is a bit of information about Emma's favourite books

Dune by Frank Herbert is about a war over the planet Arrakis, the sole source of a psychoactive drug which makes interstellar travel possible.

Three Body Problem by Cixin Liu is about humanity making contact with aliens from a trinary solar system.

Oryx and Crake by Margaret Atwood is about a dystopian future Earth ravaged by climate change and genetic engineering experiments gone wrong.
